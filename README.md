# The Pastor's Handbook

A field guide for leading a local church, written for first-time and newly appointed United Methodist pastors, Course of Study students, and First-Time Pastor's Workshop cohorts. Thirty-four chapters across eight parts teach how a congregation actually runs, week to week and year to year, with every "grab a template" moment linked to a live document on the [Church Resource Hub](https://churchresourcehub.github.io/).

The book is the map; the hub is the toolbox.

Read it at **https://churchresourcehub.github.io/pastorhandbook/**

A print edition (PDF, 180 pages) is built from the same chapters and linked from the cover page.

Built at Spanish Fort United Methodist Church on the Alabama Gulf Coast, and shared with the whole church.
